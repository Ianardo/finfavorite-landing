Trust

Built with clear boundaries.

Clear expectations around security, privacy, and data handling.

Security

  • Read-only by design. We never place trades or move money.
  • Imports happen through broker exports.
  • Your data is protected in transit.

Privacy

  • Your data is used to generate your dashboards and reports.
  • We don’t sell portfolio or personal data.
  • You decide what you upload and keep.

Data ownership

  • Your portfolio data stays exportable.
  • Download or delete your data anytime.
  • You stay in control of what’s stored.

Transparency

  • Calculations are based on your raw transactions.
  • Holdings and performance remain auditable.
  • Fix mappings and re-import when needed.
